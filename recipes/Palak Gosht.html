Palak Gosht

Description

Palak gosht is a popular curry made with lamb lathered in a spicy spinach masala.

A delicious comfort food served with chapattis, paratha, or naan.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Spinach
  • Aromatic Spices
  • Ginger and Garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • Green Chillies

Steps

  1. Add the oil in pan and heat up until hot then add the onions and cook on medium heat for 4-5 minutes until softened
  2. Add the cumin seeds, finely chopped garlic cloves, and green chilies then cook for 2-3 minutes
  3. Add the chopped tomatoes and cook for 3-4 minutes until softened
  4. Add the lamb or mutton and give everything a stir before covering and cook for 15 minutes, ensuring that you stir every few minutes
  5. Add the salt, chilli powder, turmeric powder, and coriander powder then cook the spices for 4-5 minutes
  6. As the spices are cooking finely chop the spinach then add into the pan and cover and cook for 5 minutes until the water is released
  7. Remove the lid and mix before covering and cook for 20 minutes
  8. Add the dried fenugreek and mix
  9. Serve with chapatti and enjoy!
